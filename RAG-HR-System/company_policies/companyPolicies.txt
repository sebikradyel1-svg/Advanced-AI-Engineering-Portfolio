INTERNAL POLICIES MANUAL – COMPANY XYZ

CHAPTER 1: WORKING HOURS
1.1. The standard working schedule is Monday to Friday, from 09:00 to 18:00.
1.2. The lunch break is between 13:00 and 14:00.
1.3. Working from home is allowed up to 2 days per week, with the approval of the direct manager.
1.4. Overtime must be approved in advance by the manager.

CHAPTER 2: LEAVE AND DAYS OFF
2.1. Employees are entitled to 21 working days of annual leave.
2.2. Medical leave is paid in accordance with applicable legislation.
2.3. Maternity leave is 126 calendar days.
2.4. Public holidays are paid days off.
2.5. Unpaid leave may be granted for up to 30 days per year.

CHAPTER 3: EMPLOYEE BENEFITS
3.1. Private medical insurance for all full-time employees.
3.2. Gym membership or sports activities subscription.
3.3. Meal vouchers worth 30 RON per working day.
3.4. Annual budget of 2,000 RON for training and professional development.
3.5. Annual performance-based bonus, ranging between 10–25% of salary.

CHAPTER 4: EVALUATION AND PERFORMANCE
4.1. Performance evaluations take place semi-annually, in June and December.
4.2. Objectives are set at the beginning of each year together with the manager.
4.3. Promotions are considered annually, based on performance.
4.4. Performance improvement plans last 60 days.

CHAPTER 5: CODE OF CONDUCT
5.1. Mutual respect and professionalism are mandatory.
5.2. Harassment of any kind is not tolerated.
5.3. Confidentiality of company information must be respected.
5.4. Conflicts of interest must be declared.
5.5. Dress code: business casual, except Fridays (casual).

CHAPTER 6: ADMINISTRATIVE PROCEDURES
6.1. Leave requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance.
6.2. Expense reimbursements are processed within a maximum of 30 days.
6.3. Personal data updates are handled through the HR department.
6.4. Medical certificates must be submitted within 24 hours.

CHAPTER 7: PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT
7.1. The company supports participation in relevant conferences.
7.2. Certification courses are reimbursed after successful completion.
7.3. Mentorship is available for new employees and those in development.
7.4. Department rotation is encouraged for professional growth.

CHAPTER 8: HEALTH AND SAFETY
8.1. Health and Safety (H&S) training is mandatory for all employees.
8.2. Emergency evacuations must be carried out using fire escape stairways.
8.3. Protective equipment must be worn in designated areas.
8.4. Workplace accidents must be reported immediately.
